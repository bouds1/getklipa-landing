Why Klipa is different

Regional price context.

Every deal compared against Midwest averages from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. A sale price means nothing without a baseline.

Deal memory.

Klipa remembers every price every store has run. If this week's is the best in six months, we say so. If it's not, we say that too.

Every store, side by side.

Your list, across every store we cover. One glance tells you where the trip pays off this week.
